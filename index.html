ARC (Amazing reading challenge)

The only thing I can say about it is... AMAZING!!!

Amazing Reading Challenge or ARC, is a challenge for students who love to read. Thay give you 6 books, and after you read them and practice, there's the competition. Whichever team wins, goes to the schools level, then the district, and then the state. It is amazing.
